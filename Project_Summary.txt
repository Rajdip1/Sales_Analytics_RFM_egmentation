Project Summary (Status: On-going)
Smart Demand Forecasting is an e-commerce data analysis project focused on analyzing online retail transaction data. Here's what it does:

Key Components:
Data Cleaning & Vetting (eda_1.ipynb)

Loads and validates online retail transaction data
Identifies and handles data quality issues: negative prices/quantities, missing customer IDs, incorrect datetime formats
Removes duplicates, nulls, and non-sale transactions (returns)
Creates a Revenue column for financial analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis (eda_1.ipynb & eda_2.ipynb)

Analyzes transaction patterns across different dimensions
Generates business insights like Top 10 Countries by Revenue
Explores temporal trends, product performance, and customer patterns
RFM Analysis (rfm_analysis.ipynb)

Performs Recency, Frequency, Monetary segmentation to classify customers:
Recency: Days since last purchase
Frequency: Number of transactions per customer
Monetary: Total revenue per customer
Used for customer segmentation and targeting strategies

Data Source:
The project uses online retail transaction data (~540K+ records) containing invoice details, products, quantities, prices, dates, and customer information.

This is a foundation for predictive analytics and customer relationship management (CRM) initiatives.
